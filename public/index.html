Angel Hack

AngelHack’s mission is to provide a bridge to the international technology community and the Silicon Valley. We think of coding as a new, global language for change and innovation. We teach our community how to code and that code can create empowerment, equality and open up new opportunities for all. To us, creating code together means making change, together.

AngelHack ignites the passion of the world’s most vibrant community of code creators + change makers to invent the new and make change happen, together. We help developers become change-makers in the most vibrant, global hacker community where they can build, test and launch their ideas to the world together.

We help corporate change-makers drive open innovation for products, platforms and brands by connecting them to the smarts, scale and speed of the world’s most vibrant hacker community. Our outreach initiatives are crafted to help corporate change-makers meet, greet and connect with the workforce of the future. Start a dialogue and grow your brand’s awareness among the world’s largest, most diverse and global hacker movement.
